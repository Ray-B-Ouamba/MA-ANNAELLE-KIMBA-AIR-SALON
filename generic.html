Our story

Our passion and purpose is bringing
trend-facing, modern hair to Downtown-Montreal.

Our founders grew up in Downtown-Montreal and were struck by the lack of modern salon offerings for the community upon their return - so they did something about it. Annaëlle Kimbangu-Salon opened its doors to fulfill an unmet desire for high quality, contemporary hair care in the Quebec region.

Trained and apprenticed in Downtown-Montreal,our founders envisioned Annaëlle Kimbangu-Salon to be a marriage of urban style and the SoCal vibe.
